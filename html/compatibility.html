Compatible Devices

Before you buy a Roamvy plan, make sure your device is unlocked and eSIM-compatible.

Some devices may be locked if tied to a contract or may not support eSIM, depending on the model.

 

  • You can either contact your current carrier to confirm, or insert a SIM card from another carrier — if it works, your device is unlocked.

    You can also follow the steps below based on your device type:

    iPhone:
    Go to Settings > Cellular > Add eSIM
    If you see “Add eSIM”, your device is likely unlocked and eSIM-compatible.

     

    Android:
    Go to Settings > Network & Internet > SIMs
    If you see “Add mobile plan” or “Download a SIM”, your device is likely unlocked and supports eSIM.

  • You can quickly check by dialing *#06# on your phone:

    • If you see an EID number in the results, your device supports eSIM.

    • If you only see MEID (and no EID), your device does not support eSIM.

     

    You can also check our list of popular iOS and Android devices known to be eSIM-compatible.

    Don’t see your device here? No worries! If you're unsure whether your phone supports eSIM or is unlocked, simply reach out to us for confirmation. We're here to help!
